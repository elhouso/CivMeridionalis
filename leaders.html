National Leadership

The distinguished leaders guiding Meridionalis towards prosperity

President's Office

President

Alexander Volkov

President of the Federal Republic of Meridionalis

Term: 2022 - Present (2nd term)

President Volkov was elected in 2022 after serving as Governor of Novaya Province. His administration has focused on economic modernization, infrastructure development, and strengthening Meridionalis's position in international affairs.

Vice President

Elena Petrov

Vice President of the Federal Republic of Meridionalis

Term: 2022 - Present

Former Minister of Education, Vice President Petrov champions social welfare and education initiatives. She oversees the National Space Program and chairs the Council on Science and Technology.
