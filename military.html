Armed Forces of Meridionalis

Defending the sovereignty and territorial integrity of the Federal Republic

Defense Overview

Mission Statement

The Armed Forces of Meridionalis exist to defend the sovereignty and territorial integrity of the Federal Republic, protect its citizens, and contribute to international peace and security through alliances and partnerships.

Core Values

  • Duty: To serve the nation with honor and integrity
  • Excellence: To maintain the highest standards of professionalism
  • Loyalty: To the Constitution and the people of Meridionalis
  • Courage: Physical and moral bravery in all circumstances

Defense Strategy

Territorial Defense

Maintain capability to defend against external threats to Meridionalis sovereignty and territory.

Alliance Commitments

Fulfill obligations to the Northern Defense Pact and other international security agreements.

Cyber Security

Protect critical infrastructure from digital threats and maintain cyber warfare capabilities.

Peacekeeping

Contribute to international peacekeeping operations under UN mandate.

Technological Edge

Invest in next-generation military technologies to maintain qualitative advantage.

Civil Resilience

Enhance national resilience against hybrid threats and emergencies.

Meridionalis Army

The Meridionalis Army is the land warfare branch of the Armed Forces, responsible for territorial defense and ground operations. With a history dating back to the nation's founding, today's army is a modern, professional force equipped with advanced weapons systems and trained for full-spectrum operations.

90,000 Reserves
