Title I, Chapter 1 - Definitions

Last amended: June 12, 2021 Code: MRD-GEN-001 Effective: January 1, 2020

Section 1.01 - Definitions

For the purposes of this Code and all laws of Meridionalis, unless the context otherwise requires:

  1. "Person" includes individuals, corporations, partnerships, associations, and other legal entities recognized by law.
  2. "State" means any of the constituent states of the Federal Republic of Meridionalis.
  3. "Federal Government" refers to the national government of the Federal Republic of Meridionalis.
  4. "Law" means any constitutional provision, statute, ordinance, regulation, or judicial decision having the force of law in Meridionalis.
  5. "Public Official" means any person holding a position of trust or authority in the government of Meridionalis at any level.

Section 1.02 - Interpretation

  1. Words importing the singular include the plural, and words importing the plural include the singular.
  2. Words of one gender include all other genders.
  3. The headings of sections and subsections are for convenience only and shall not affect the construction or interpretation of this Code.
