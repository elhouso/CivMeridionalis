Major Industries

Technology

Meridionalis has become a regional leader in software development, electronics manufacturing, and renewable energy technologies. The "Silicon Valley" of Meridionalis is located in Novagrad's tech district.

Contribution to GDP: 18% Annual Growth: 7.2%

Manufacturing

The nation has a strong manufacturing base, particularly in automotive, aerospace, and precision machinery. The Eastern Industrial Zone is home to several major manufacturing hubs.

Contribution to GDP: 22% Annual Growth: 3.8%

Financial Services

Novagrad's financial district is the largest in the region, with a thriving banking sector, stock exchange, and growing fintech industry.

Contribution to GDP: 15% Annual Growth: 4.5%

Agriculture

Despite urbanization, Meridionalis remains a major agricultural producer, especially in grains, fruits, and dairy products from its fertile central plains.
